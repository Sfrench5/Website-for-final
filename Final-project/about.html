It started with an addiction to great coffee and a dream to one day own a cafe that brought delicious food and comfy place to enjoy it. That dream finally became a reality when Coffee Cafe first opened its doors to the wonderful residents of Bucktown.

We've now grown larger than we've ever expected to be and let our success thus far push us to keep standards high and the coffee great. Great drinks, food, and treats are part of the recipe for our success but where we truly shine is our friendly and wonderful staff. Combine them with a cozy atmosphere and delicious food and it's easy to see why Bucktown chooses us

Now that we are we've made it this far, it only felt right that we should try to give back to the town we love so much. We've partnered up with local charities and food drives to donate anything we can that would otherwise go to waste. We believe in Bucktown and want all of our residents to be able to enjoy, even those who may not be able to afford to.

Coffee Cafe is also pushing a no-waste initiative which includes reduction of plastics. So all of our beverages will now be served with strawless or with paper straws. Plastic straws are still available upon request. We want to do our part to contribute and help keep our planet going.
